Project Proposal

Project Concept/Motivation:

  • I would like to combine this project with HCDE 438 to play the Mario Game. Joystick with x and y axis to control Mario move right, left or jump. Make a LED strip light up when the player gets a high score, but the LCD will mock the user and LED strip will light down when the player gets a low score.

Anticipated Bill of Materials:

  • LED Strips
  • P5.JS
  • Joystick
  • 9V battery power supply
  • Transistor for current limiting
  • LCD
